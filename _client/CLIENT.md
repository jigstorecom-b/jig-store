# Client Brief

## Business Name
Jig

## Industry
E-commerce / Online Marketplace

## What They Do
Jig is a modern e-commerce platform that enables merchants to list, manage, and sell
products online. It serves both independent sellers and growing brands who want a
fast, beautiful storefront without the bloat of legacy platforms.

## Target Audience
- Independent sellers and small businesses
- Growing DTC (direct-to-consumer) brands
- Shoppers who value clean, fast, modern online stores

## Primary Goal of the Website
Enable merchants to sell products online — seamless product browsing, cart, and checkout.

## Pages Required
- Home (hero, featured products, categories, trust signals)
- Shop / Product Listing
- Product Detail Page
- Cart
- Checkout
- Order Confirmation
- Merchant Dashboard (orders, products, analytics)
- About Jig
- Contact / Support

## Key Features
- Product listings with variants (size, colour, etc.)
- Shopping cart and checkout flow
- Paystack payment integration
- Order management
- Media uploads for product images
- SEO-optimised product pages
- Mobile-first responsive design

## What They Do NOT Need
- Webinars
- Bookings / appointment scheduling
- Blog (Phase 1 — add in Phase 2 if needed)
- Multi-currency (Phase 1 — single currency)
