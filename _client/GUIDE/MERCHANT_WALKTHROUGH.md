# Merchant Walkthrough: Managing Your Store

This guide walk you through the day-to-day operations of your Jig store using the Admin Panel.

## 1. Adding a New Product

1. Log in to `yourdomain.com/admin`.
2. Go to **Products** > **Add New**.
3. **Name & Slug:** Enter the product name. The slug (the URL) will auto-generate.
4. **Price:** Set your price in Naira (₦). Use the "Compare Price" if the item is on sale.
5. **Category:** Select a category from the dropdown (ensure you've created categories first!).
6. **Variants:** Add variants like "Size" or "Colour". 
   - *Example:* Name: "Large", Stock: 10.
7. **Media:** Upload a high-resolution "Main Product Image" and additional shots for the gallery.
8. **Status:** Set to **Published** to make it live.

## 2. Organizing Categories

Categories power the horizontal strip on your homepage.
1. Go to **Categories** > **Add New**.
2. Give it a name and a slug.
3. Check the **Featured** box if you want this category to appear in the top-level navigation strip.

## 3. Handling Orders

When a customer checks out:
1. You will receive an email notification (once configured).
2. The order will appear in the **Orders** collection.
3. **Reviewing:** Click an order to see the customer's email, the items they bought, and the total paid.
4. **Fulfillment:** Once you've shipped the item, change the **Status** to "Fulfilled".

## 4. Editing Homepage Content

You don't need to touch code to change your homepage text.
1. Go to **Pages**.
2. Find the page with the slug `home`.
3. Update the **Hero Heading** (e.g., "The Summer Collection") and **Hero Subtext**.
4. Save the changes, and the site will update instantly.

## 5. Media Management

The **Media** collection stores all your uploads.
- **Optimization:** Try to keep images under 500KB for the fastest page loads.
- **Consistency:** Use a consistent aspect ratio (e.g., 4:5 vertical) for product shots to keep the grid looking sharp.
